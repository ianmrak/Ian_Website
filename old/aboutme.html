Who am I?

Hi! My name's Ian Mrak. I'm 26 years old and in the final stages of completing my MFA in 3D Character Animation.

I grew up on a horse farm outside of Little Rock, Arkansas. When I wasn't outside I was engrossed in any digital medium I could get my hands on because it was sometimes difficult making the drive into town to see friends. My grade school had a quality art program and was able to provide digital art software which sparked my initial interest. I also had several friends who were interested in learning programs such as Photoshop and Illustrator which helped further my curiosity about graphic art.

Despite this, my focus in college ended up being Biology with the intent on moving on to medical school. Both my parents worked in the scientific field and I enjoyed learning. Thus I graduated in 2011 with a B.S. in Biology from the University of Arkansas. However, during the final stages on my undergrad I was having difficulty imagining myself working in the medical field and after repeated discussions from friends and family, decided to change direction.

I then packed up and moved to San Francisco where I spent a year pursuing an MFA in Game Design at the Academy of Art University. I fell in love with Maya and especially enjoyed creating environments. My decision to change tracks was largely influenced by an environment modeling class I took that focused heavily on Maya and ZBrush. I came to realized my preference to design over game mechanics in my classes, switch to the Animation program and haven't looked back!
